A short history

Twenty years, told briefly.

From a small Vegas garage to one of the largest paint booths in Nevada.

  1. 2005

    Spray Gun opens

    Ofer Nurkin opens Spray Gun Auto Body in Las Vegas with a focus on quality collision and paint work.

  2. 2008

    First fleet contract

    Picks up first commercial fleet account — a relationship that snowballs into UPS, FedEx, Penske and more.

  3. 2012

    65 ft spray booth installed

    The biggest booth in the valley — wide enough for semi-trailers, containers, water tanks, full RVs.

  4. 2016

    Nellis AFB partnership

    Begins servicing Nellis Air Force Base vehicles. The military discount program launches.

  5. 2020

    Free pickup & delivery valley-wide

    Adds licensed CDL drivers and flatbeds. Customers stop driving in.

  6. 2025

    Twenty years strong

    Three generations of Vegas families bringing their cars back. Thousands of vehicles repaired. Still the same owner answering the phone.
