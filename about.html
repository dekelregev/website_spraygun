A short history

Thirty years, told briefly.

From a small Vegas garage to one of the largest paint booths in Nevada.

  1. 1996

    Ofer enters the trade

    Ofer Nurkin starts his career in auto body & collision repair in Las Vegas — learning the craft from the ground up.

  2. 2001

    First shop

    Opens his first independent shop in Las Vegas. Word spreads fast — quality work, honest pricing, owner on the floor every day.

  3. 2005

    The Spray Gun opens

    Launches Spray Gun Auto Body at 4245 Production Ct — the shop that becomes his home for the next two decades and counting.

  4. 2008

    First fleet contract

    Picks up first commercial fleet account — a relationship that snowballs into UPS, FedEx, Penske, Pepsi, Coca-Cola and more.

  5. 2012

    65 ft spray booth installed

    The biggest booth in the valley — wide enough for semi-trailers, containers, water tanks, full RVs. No other Vegas shop comes close.

  6. 2016

    Nellis AFB partnership

    Begins servicing Nellis Air Force Base vehicles. The military discount program launches.

  7. 2020

    Free pickup & delivery valley-wide

    Adds licensed CDL drivers and flatbeds. Customers never have to drive in — Spray Gun comes to them.

  8. 2026

    Thirty years strong

    Generations of Vegas families still bringing their cars back. Thousands of vehicles repaired. Same owner. Same number. Same promise.
